About Us

This is a website created for people who enjoy the movie My Neighbor Totoro, a Japanese animated film by Hayao Miyazaki and Studio Ghibli. It follows the story of the Kusakabes as they move to a rural village to be closer to their mother who is in the hospital recovering. The girls, Satsuki and Mei, meet some forest Spirits including Totoro and develop a deeper connection and appreciation for nature.

Everyone is welcomed here!
